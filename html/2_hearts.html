2 Hearts (2020)

Cast

Radha Mitchell, Jacob Elordi, Kari Matchett, Adan Canto

Overview

2 Hearts is a 2020 American romantic drama film directed by Lance Hool and starring Jacob Elordi, Adan Canto, Tiera Skovbye and Radha Mitchell. It is based on the true story of Leslie and Jorge Bacardi and Christopher Gregory. The film was theatrically released in the United States on October 16, 2020, receiving generally negative reviews from critics.

When illness strikes two people who are polar opposites, life and death bring them together in surprising ways.

Two different couples in different decades and different places share a hidden connection that eventually brings them together. The story is narrated by Chris Gregory, one of the main characters in the film. He shares the story of the first couple, stating that their stories are connected to one another.
